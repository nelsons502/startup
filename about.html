    About Us

    LongboardLinks was created to bring together students at Brigham Young University who use other forms of transportation on campus other than their feet or a motor vehicle.

    Here you can find and create boarding events in and near Provo, UT. You can also post pictures of yourself and your buddies enjoying time together on your boards.

    LongboardLinks is created, run, and maintained by Nelson Schnepf. Feel free to contact him at nmschnepf@gmail.com!
